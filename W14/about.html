About Us


We have been working in the technology space for over 15 years, and we have recently combined a love of art with our interest in artificial intelligence and machine learning. We have been working on a project to create art using AI and machine learning, and this site is the first version of our project. We hope you enjoy the art and the site.

We are actively working on implementing several of the other AI art algorithms that we discussed on the algorithms page. We are also working to tap into existing art databases, art APIs and AI art APIs to create a more robust art experience. These include the Harvard Art API, Dall-e, and Stable Diffusion.
